# Disclaimer

This MCP server provides Swedish agricultural grant and subsidy data for informational purposes only.

## Not Financial or Legal Advice

The data served by this tool does not constitute financial, legal, or grant application advice. Grant eligibility criteria, payment rates, deadlines, and stacking rules are based on published Swedish government sources, but individual eligibility depends on the applicant's specific circumstances. Always verify current grant availability and eligibility with Jordbruksverket or the relevant Lansstyrelsen before committing resources to an application.

## Data Sources

All data is sourced from:

- **Jordbruksverket** (Swedish Board of Agriculture) -- Investment support, environmental investments, young farmer schemes, payment rates
- **Tillvaxtverket** (Swedish Agency for Economic and Regional Growth) -- Rural development project grants, broadband support
- **Lansstyrelsen** (County Administrative Boards) -- Regional grants, predator protection, environmental measures

Data is derived from Swedish public authority publications.

## Currency

Grant schemes open and close. Budgets are exhausted, deadlines pass, and new grant rounds are announced. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run. Run `check_data_freshness` to see the age of the data. Always check Jordbruksverket e-tjanster for current open rounds and deadlines.

## Jurisdiction

This server covers Sweden (SE) only. EU CAP Strategic Plan funding is reflected through Swedish implementation (Jordbruksverket), not the EU-level programme directly.
